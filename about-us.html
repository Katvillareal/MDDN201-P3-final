Welcome to this Travel blog

Hi guys, I'm 22 years old and I have been living in Los Angeles my entire life. Once I finished college, I did not want to go to University. Instead, I found a job and started working retail! After 3 years of retail, I got so bored for doing the same thing every day. I decided, to move back in with my parents and start saving. I wanted to get out of Los Angeles. I saved majority of my pay checks. Once I had enough, I started planning, planning to travel the world and meet new people.

I wanted to first make my way around South America, then I made my way around Europe!! I visited the most beautiful places! I then made my way around Asia. I was able to experience all different cultures and meet amazing people that have now joined me. I wanted to share my experiences with my friends and family. My blog lets you read about all my experiences, what amazing foods I've been eating and any struggles and obstacles that I had to come across!
